Travel

As a child, my parents always liked to bring me and my sibings to places that werent so typical. We spent a lot of time in the Carribean, my favourite being Barbados. We did Paris and st.Tropez, Malaga and Lanzarote. Without a doubt, Barbados is somewhere I will return to. The azure blue sea and the white sand beaches matched with the warm breeze and hot temperatures make it one of my favourite places on earth. One of most foond memories is the time we went swimming with turtles.One afternoon after lunch, we took aa boat out to a pier which was being surrounded by turtles. It was such an incredible experience.I swam right next to them as we fed them pieces of fish and it was truly something I will never forget. Besides the temperatures and beaches, the kindness and generosity of the people will always be very memourable too. I cannot wait to return in the future.
